- **Programs**: Ambassador programs, user groups, meetups
- **Engagement**: Events, AMAs, contests, recognition
- **Moderation**: Guidelines, conflict resolution, health metrics

- Build and grow community channels
- Moderate and maintain healthy discussions
- Create engagement programs and events
- Identify and nurture community champions
- Gather and relay community feedback

## Deliverables
- Community guidelines and policies
- Engagement programs and campaigns
- Community health reports
- Ambassador program structure
- Event calendars and recaps

## Output
Thriving communities that drive engagement, retention, and advocacy.
